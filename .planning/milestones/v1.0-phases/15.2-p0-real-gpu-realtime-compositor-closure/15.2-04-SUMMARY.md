- the desktop binding/host attaches Rust surface state and no longer uses the native video bridge;
- the compositor can sample registered WGPU NV12 plane leases;
- raw macOS CoreVideo/Metal leases are still registered as platform resources that cannot be wrapped into WGPU sampleable textures through the currently exposed API;
- no Rust-owned scheduler currently decodes timeline media into sampleable leases, builds frame-provider state, calls `present_to_surface`, and records `renderGraphGpuComposited` evidence during normal play.
