# Phase 10.1 Plan 03: Playhead Seek And Preview Requests Summary

**Jianying-style playhead seeking now works from time inputs, frame-step buttons, ruler clicks, and playhead dragging, with preview frames requested at the exact integer-microsecond seek target.**

## Accomplishments

- Centralized seek behavior in `App.tsx` so seek-driven controls normalize target time, update `playheadUs`, and request `requestPreviewFrame` for that same target.
- Updated preview previous/next frame controls to use `draft.canvasConfig.frameRate` rational values, including `30000/1001` frame stepping.
- Added ruler click and playhead drag interactions in the timeline that compute integer microsecond targets from pointer geometry without mutating draft semantics.
- Added Playwright coverage for preview-time input, rational frame stepping, ruler click, playhead drag, and observable `requestPreviewFrame.targetTime` values.

## Decisions Made

- Seek side effects are centralized in `App.tsx`; children report target times but do not own preview request side effects.
- Frame stepping rounds `denominator * 1_000_000 / numerator` to integer microseconds from the accepted draft canvas frame rate.
- Timeline pointer seeking is display-only and command-free, preserving Rust ownership of draft/timeline semantics.
