- Added product tests that reject commit-only timeline ghosts, scrub undo/save pollution, absolute/floating keyframe times, visible unsupported transition/fade handles, and exact-playhead-only keyframe deletion.

## Decisions Made

- Transition/fade handles are not exposed as inert UI. They stay hidden until typed transition/fade commands, preview, export, and undo semantics exist.
- Keyframe marker visual movement may use a local marker-position affordance for immediate pointer feedback, but live update samples and product evidence must come from Rust `keyframeEdit` observations and preview invalidation deltas.
- Keyed visual sliders do not mutate the segment visual directly when a matching property keyframe exists; they edit the nearest selected property keyframe through Rust.
