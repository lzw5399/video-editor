## Decisions Made

- Product presentation target creation fails closed for offscreen and mock descriptors, even though those remain valid diagnostic/test concepts.
- `WgpuSurfacePresent` is distinct from `WgpuRenderPass`; the latter can still be an offscreen diagnostic readback path, while the former means a surface texture was presented.
- Surface presentation output intentionally uses `pixels: None` so CPU readback cannot be mistaken for product playback evidence.
